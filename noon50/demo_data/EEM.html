      Fund Summary

      The investment seeks to track the investment results of the MSCI Emerging Markets Index. The underlying index is composed of large- and mid-capitalization emerging market equities. The fund generally invests at least 90% of its assets in the securities of its underlying index and in depositary receipts representing securities in its underlying index. View More
